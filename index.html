Saylani Welfare Trust is a beacon of hope and compassion, illuminating the lives of countless individuals across Pakistan. Founded in May 1999 by Maulana Bashir Farooqui, this non-governmental organization (NGO) has been dedicated to serving humanity with unwavering commitment and selfless devotion. At the heart of Saylani Welfare Trust's mission is the belief that every human being deserves dignity, compassion, and opportunity. With this guiding principle, the Trust has embarked on a multifaceted journey to alleviate poverty, provide healthcare, offer education, and empower communities. One of the most remarkable aspects of Saylani Welfare Trust is its comprehensive approach to social welfare. The Trust operates numerous programs and initiatives, ranging from food distribution and healthcare services to education programs and skills development. Through its flagship initiatives such as the Saylani Mass Training & Job Creation Program, the Trust has empowered thousands of individuals by equipping them with valuable skills and providing them with employment opportunities. Food distribution lies at the core of Saylani Welfare Trust's humanitarian efforts. The Trust's Saylani Dastarkhwan initiative ensures that no one goes to bed hungry by providing free meals to the underprivileged across Pakistan. With a network of Dastarkhwans spanning multiple cities, Saylani Welfare Trust serves millions of meals every month, bringing nourishment and hope to those in need. Moreover, Saylani Welfare Trust's healthcare services play a pivotal role in improving the well-being of communities. From mobile medical camps in remote areas to state-of-the-art medical facilities, the Trust ensures access to quality healthcare for all. Additionally, the Trust's Saylani Hospital provides free medical treatment and surgeries, serving as a lifeline for those who cannot afford healthcare expenses. Education is another cornerstone of Saylani Welfare Trust's initiatives. Through its schools, vocational training centers, and scholarship programs, the Trust empowers individuals with education and skills, enabling them to break the cycle of poverty and build a better future for themselves and their families. In essence, Saylani Welfare Trust stands as a shining example of compassion, generosity, and service to humanity. With its tireless efforts and unwavering dedication, the Trust continues to make a profound impact on the lives of the underprivileged, spreading rays of hope and kindness in every corner of Pakistan
